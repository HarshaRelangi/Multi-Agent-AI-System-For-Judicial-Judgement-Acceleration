                    EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT
=================================================================

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on 
January 15, 2024, between ABC Corporation ("Employer") and 
John Doe ("Employee").

ARTICLE I - POSITION AND DUTIES
Employee shall serve as Senior Software Developer, reporting 
to Jane Smith, Director of Engineering.

ARTICLE II - COMPENSATION
Employee shall receive an annual salary of $95,000, payable 
in bi-weekly installments.

ARTICLE III - TERM OF EMPLOYMENT
This Agreement shall commence on February 1, 2024 and shall 
continue indefinitely, subject to termination as provided herein.

ARTICLE IV - TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written 
notice. Termination may occur immediately in case of breach 
of contract terms.

ARTICLE V - CONFIDENTIALITY
Employee agrees to maintain confidentiality of all proprietary 
information and trade secrets of the Company.

ARTICLE VI - NON-COMPETE
Employee agrees not to compete with the Employer within 50 miles 
for a period of 12 months following termination.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement 
on the date first written above.
